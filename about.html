About Team Martians

Born and raised in Philadelphia, I went to high school in Rhode Island, college in Maine, and worked on Wall Street out of college. After a few years in New York City, I moved to Seattle to try something new. I started working in high-tech startups as soon as I arrived. Since then, I've conceived of, launched, and marketed B2B and B2C products for most of my career. But now I want more control to build my own applications. Thus, I'm here at CodeFellows to learn Javascript, HTML, and CSS.

My name is Hawa, it means 'air' in both Hindi and Arabic. I am a mid-western at heart, but after moving here to Seattle in 2010, Seattle is growing on me slowly but surely. I am currently attending Code Fellows in hopes of becoming a full-stack web developer. I majored in Early Childhood and Elementary Education at the teacher preparation program at Central Washington University. With my background of Education and the skills I gained from CF, I hope to combine my two passions into one very soon.
